Fig. 2 | Perception and representation module for physical–electromagnetic world modelling.
The WM-metaEI perception module converts an unseen indoor environment into a simulation-ready physical–electromagnetic world model. Intelligent metasurface (IMS) poses are recovered from camera observations through feature matching, geometric verification and homography-based pose estimation, while radio-frequency source positions are inferred from channel state information using SpotFi-based AoA/ToF estimation and multi-view localization. Autonomous exploration and R3LIVE mapping generate dense RGB-coloured point clouds, which are further parsed by SpatialLM into semantic object entities and by RoomFormer into room-level topology. Semantic objects are then assigned electromagnetic material properties according to the ITU-R P.2040 library. The resulting representation integrates geometry, objects, room topology, material parameters, IMS poses and RF-source positions for downstream electromagnetic prediction and metasurface optimization.
