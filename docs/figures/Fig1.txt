Fig. 1 | Architecture and operational logic of WM-metaEI.
Schematic overview of the World-Model Metasurface Embodied Intelligence (WM-metaEI) framework. The system integrates three functional modules: a heterogeneous perception and representation module that constructs a semantic physical–electromagnetic world model of the environment; an electromagnetic (EM) dynamics prediction module that simulates radio-wave propagation under candidate metasurface states; and a decision-and-execution module that converts user-level instructions into hardware-executable information metasurface (IMS) coding patterns. Driven by a vision–language interface, WM-metaEI links natural-language commands, digital-twin-based EM prediction and programmable metasurface control, enabling out-of-the-box operation in previously unseen indoor environments.
