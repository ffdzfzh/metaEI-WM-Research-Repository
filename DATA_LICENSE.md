# Data License Status

The datasets in `data/` are provided for manuscript review and result inspection. No general permission to redistribute, modify, or use these data for unrelated purposes is granted in this pre-publication version.

Before the archival public release, the authors should:

1. confirm that every image, video, point cloud, and measurement can be made public;
2. remove personal, location-sensitive, or device-identifying metadata;
3. select an explicit data license, such as CC BY 4.0 or CC BY-NC 4.0;
4. deposit large data in a durable research archive with a DOI; and
5. replace this file with the final data-license text and archive citation.

Files derived from third-party datasets or models remain subject to any additional upstream terms.
